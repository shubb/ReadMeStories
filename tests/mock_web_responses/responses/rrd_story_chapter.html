[Eyes slowly opening]


After going back to sleep last night, it seems that it’s now morning and everyone has woken up. Well at least I’m in the clear for now. I just have to blend in with everyone else and when the time comes I'll just leave and live on my own. Although that will probably be 15 years from now, shit!


I get up and realize I can not move my entire body. I started looking around--


The hell is this? Why am I and everyone else, who were the size a human baby, around 4ft tall?!?


After coming to grips with being this big, yet only 1 day old the elder that I saw last night walked into the cave like room.


Unknown: “Listen up! Everyone follow me!”


The lady shouted with an indifferent face.


After 3mins of walking in what is definitely a cave we arrived at a room. In front of me and the other 12 newborn babies was a big king like chair, it didn't have any fancy jewel or gems on it but was simply huge. It’s backrest stood around 9ft tall and there was a black scaled “man” sitting in it. The “man’s” name was Vulca.


Vulca: “Welcome children. My name is Vulca, but you may call me elder. All 13 of you are the sons and daughter of the Crondia tribe, but you have not been accepted by us. You will undergo a trial. This trial will prove that you are indeed members of our great tribe and will have the strength and wit to survival in this world.”


Vulca lifted his hand up and motioned to the female.


Vulca: “Listen well children. You will choose one weapon and once you have chosen your weapon your trial will begin. In the forest outside of this cave is home to many dangerous and violent monsters. But there is only monster that you must defeat and bring back to complete your trial. It’s called the Rugtian, a small but nimble and fierce monster that will have you for lunch if you are not careful. Bring back the corpse of 1 Rugtian and you will be accepted into the Crondia tribe.”


After the elder gave us the rough outline of our trial he gave us a detailed description of the Rugtian. It apparently has ruby red eyes accompanied by it slender body that’s around 3ft tall. It walks on it 2 back legs and harbor razor sharp claws in it front paws. The elder warned us that the it could blend in with the forest, but as for the specifics like its color he didn’t mention.


Anyway, so I have to beat this trial if I ever want to have a conversation with anyone in this tribe. It’s can’t be that hard, right? I’ve played my fair share of games in my lif--previous life, but I never really committed long term to any specific one. Why, well they got very easy and boring, when you're hundreds of levels over the NPC’s and monsters, well what’s the point in the game?


Were being led to a small room, I think, that has a wooden door.


Unknown: “Each of you will take turns choosing 1 weapon from this room.”


The women then entered the room and a few seconds she shouted out for the first child to enter the room.


… … …


After 7 children had gone into the room and came back out with what I can only consider the shittiest look sword, bow, spear, or whatever weapon they chose THEY ALL LOOKED LIKED A METAL TIED TO A STICK! Even medieval Europe had more advance weapons! Do they not have blacksmiths in this village, c'mon at least give me a real sword!


My turn had arrived and I entered the room. There behind a long table stood the unknown woman, what’s her name I wondered. She has been our guide for our entire lives! She deserved a thank you gift at least, if you know what I mean hey it's not like i had anything else to offer and relatively speaking if was that small.


… … …


Okay so the jist of it is like this: they don't want to waste any precious material on us because there's probably a high mortality rate for this trial, shit! I started getting a little anxious, but the fact remained the their were real swords and cool looking weapons in the armory room, but we couldn't use them.


What weapon did I choose? I didn’t really care about my choice as there was no way in hell I was actually going to use the weapon. I had two options either make my own weapon or “borrow” a real weapon. Unfortunately the female didn’t look like she was going to move from behind the table so I went with option 1.


As I made my way outside the cave, I saw quite a few doors that looked like rooms that people lived in.
I arrived at the exit of the cave and my eyes began to adjust to the bright light and looking around there was what seemed to be a village just outside the cave. Anyway I started walking straight till I hit the forest. I made sure not to stop and get distracted, because no one would talk to me so trying would be wasted effort.


I got to the entrance of the forest, after passing what seem to be a wooden gate, that enclosed the village in a wooden spiked fence. After 5mins of wandering around I decided to take a rest and make a plan, but in the corner of my eye I spotted a snake like tail that seemed to be behind me. Almost instantly i turned my neck and was about to give out a scream, when I noticed that there was no monster behind me, matter of fact the tail seemed to becoming from my lower back. I freaked out, stunned at fact that I had a tail, and realized I don't feel it and control it! What the hell?!? Why is it moving by itself?!??!!? I got a little worried about my tail that seemed to have a mind of it’s own, but I grabbed it and coiled it around my wait hoping it wouldn't get in my way


Anyway, I also remember the scale like skin that the elder and women had and it finally hit me to look at myself. My skin was black like the void, my figure was that of a slender 4ft tall boy that was also only 1 day old and already could speak a language. Back to my description the scale like skin seemed to be like a rattlesnakes but much smoother. As for my face I didn’t have a mirror so I had no clue what my hair, eyes, and other facial features looked like, but now wasn't the time to be worrying about my looks.


I ended up throwing away the poor excuse for a sword away in favor of a fairly big tree branch that I found. It size was around that of firewood and if you were struck on in head with it you’d be out cold.


I rummaged around the forest a bit till I caught a glimpse of a 5ft snake feeding on a boar it had caught. I slowly approached the snake and waited till it had swallowed the boar before making my attack.


Okay, let’s face the facts! First I was never an athletic guy. Second I looked like some crazy lunatic who had been trying to catch bigfoot and finally found him.


I started running as fast as I could, in a few seconds I was around 10ft away from the snake. As I kept running I slowly raised up my firewood like batton and dropped it straight onto the snake's head.


The snake started rushing to gulp down the boar before Jake arrived in front of him, but the firewood fell upon his head before he could even put up an ounce of resistance.


I started smashing the snakes face in with the firewood till I could clearly see blood and verified that snake was indeed dead.


I took a short breather to regain my composure after looking like a psycho murdering a helpless creature, but I didn’t care. I knew that If I was going to live in this world, then I would have to change my original way of life, that included eating whatever I killed because no one was going to give me any food, especially not for free.


I didn’t have a knife or sharp object so I just straight up bit into the snake's body chewing and chewing at the meat mixed with slimy skin, but it didn’t taste that bad.


After finishing up my meal I continued my hunt for the Rugtian.


I came upon a small stream that looked like it contained clean water so I took a sip. After refreshing myself in the stream I decided to follow the stream downwards in hopes that a rugtian was also having a drink.


After following the stream for what felt like forever(aka 10 mins) I came upon a 3ft tall, two legged monster that was clothed in dark blue rough fur. It had small ears and looked like something out of a low budget cgi hollywood movie. A deformed face that lacked any hair or fur that truly would have scared the shit out of you.


I needed to make a plan if I was going to kill this thing. First It’s not handicapped like the snake, so no straight up charge. Second it was probably a lot fast than me, so I need a plan that slowed him down, while giving me enough time to land a hit.


BINGO!!


I noticed it’s footprints and decided to take a gamble. I was going to live in this world but also might as well fulfil some of my dreams. I was going to copy a certain video game that I once played.


I circled the horrific looking blue fur monster till I was directly about 100ft behind him.


I looked around for a good position to complete my assassination as it would put me in his blind spot and give me extra brute force, courtesy of gravity. As I climbed the tree I tried to make as little noise as possible and somehow managed to not be found.


I got into position. I was ready to leap at the ugly little fucker any time.


… … …


I waited.


I waited some more….. and some more.


What the fuck?!?! Why isn't it moving??? I was beginning to run out of patience. I waited for over 40mins, yet it did not return. I could clearly see it through the tree branches but it did not return and only stood still staring at the stream.


I’m about to jump down right now and run this motherfu--


*rustle* rustle*


I looked down and there was clearly another little furry fucker walking toward the stream. Is that why it was waiting?


Shit! Shit! Shit!


At this point it was already around 6pm and If I didn't return before sundown with a rugtian, I was probably going to get killed by the tribe or exiled, which would be worse because I don’t know shit about shit!


I either act now or never! I steeled up my nerves and as the second rugtian was approaching my strike zone on his way to the stream I jumped.


As gravity sped me up I arc’d my back and brought my firewood as hard as I could down on to it’s head.


*Thump* *Splash*


As the wooden club crushed the rugtian’s skull blood and brains spurted out all over the surrounding forest. Although I had finally killed a rugtian and succeeded in completing my goal, there was no way that the other rugtian by the stream was going to let me go without a fight. How did I know that? As I slowly got up after the 15ft jump the other rugtian was staring at me 10ft away. With eyes that shined deeper and scarier than any red I’ve ever seen I realize that I was in deep shit!


*Starring*


After starring at each other for 10-20secs the rugtian jumped at me. I tried to dive to my right but it’s left paw’s sharp claws ripped through my left shoulder. I felt like someone had hit me with 1000 sewing needles in one concentrated area. I toughen myself up knowing that pain had no room in my brain in this fight. I quickly turned to my left and swung the wooden baton with my right hand. Like a batter hitting an over-sized baseball the baton smashed into the furry fucker stomach as it was sent flying into the ground a few feet away. This world wasn’t like before, I had a fast mind and a fast body compared to my human life it was 2 to 3 times stronger and faster. I could fight this monster if I used both brain and brawn carefully. The fury fucker got up and immediately charge me again.


Jake muffled under his breath: “No you don’t!”


*Smash*


The wooden batten like a bullet smashed in the furry fuckers face. Jake had spun himself around and threw the wooden batten at it’s face. The timing for the hit was nothing for Jake he calculated everything before it could even get up.


Now the little furry fucker was squealing on the ground in front of Jake. The baton indented a circular mark into it’s face. Both it’s eye were no longer functioning and looked like pulverized meatballs. Jake approached the poor guy and picked up the wooden baton. He raised it over his head and with one final blow the little guy was put out of his suffering.


Just like with the snake he decided to eat one of them. He was already starving even after eating himself full with snake. Jake couldn't return with 2 rugtians or that would go against his wish to blend in till he could live on his own.


Come to think of it, with this body's growth rate it’ll probably take less than 2-3 years before he could live by himself.


… 2 hours later...


With the rugtian on his back Jake approached the entrance of the cave. He had received many glances while on his way back to the cave, but this time they didn’t have the same amount of hostility toward him.


I entered the cave and went straight to the elder Vulca room. As I entered I looked around and saw 4 other children, 1 girl and 3 boys. I approached the table that had the other dead rugtian’s on it and placed mine on the table and retreated to the wall were the other 4 were. After a few minutes Vulca entered the room with a radiant smile.


Vulca: “Nicely done children! The trial is over and you 5 are the only ones who have passed”


Vulca made his way to the giant chair and sat down.


Vulca: “Many of you have many questions and all will be answered in time. But there is one thing left for you to complete. Your Blessed Awakening.”


Vulca: “Once you’ve awakened you blessings, you will officially be recognized as a member of the crondia tribe. You will also release your inner magical power, which will grant you the ability to use magic.”


Vulca: “The blessed awakening must be performed alone. Lizeza will guide you to the altar.”


As Vulca finished his words the beautiful women that guided us in the morning walked into the room.


Lizeza: “Congratulations on your completion of the Coming of Age trial. Please follow me.” in a polite tone


Wait!?! Did she say COMING OF AGE TRIAL???? Does that mean were already adults now??? Were fucking 2 day old and we have to live on our own already??? Pissed off and annoyed at what he had just heard he was about to explode in a rage of complaints when Lizeza started leaving the room.


They walked deeper into the cave till they came to a small room with shelfs full of small bottles and herbs. Lizeza took 5 bottles off the shelf and handed them to the 5 children.


Lizeza: “Drink this for your wounds.”


After gulping down the bottle of vinegar tasting water a pain that Jake desperately tried to bury resurfaced into his mind.


Ahhhh!! Aghhhhh!! That’s right, my shoulder!! Jake looked down at his left shoulder as it slowly began to close.


What the hell is this? It’s disappearing! My wound is almost gone!


After another few minutes of walking they arrived at a bright white door that had a weird magic circle on it.


Lizeza: “Once you enter the altar you awakening will begin.”


After that Lizeza started walking back the way we came.


You could at least be a little nicer! Sheesh! Man I hope not all the people here are like that.


Eventually all 5 of the kids entered the room. The door wasn’t a physical door but a magical door, so there is 5 children in 5 different rooms at one time.

Jake enters a dimly lit room with marble flooring. The wall’s had strange symbols, probably magical circles like the door. Jake approached the altar, in front of him was a faint blue ball of energy. Jake placed his hands onto the ball and his focus was pulled away from the physical world into his magical essence.

Suddenly something appeared in his mind


Status Configured



After that another message popped up





Status Window

Name: [Decide Name]
Age: Less Than 1 Year Old

Blessing(s):

"Origin"
"?????????"
"????????"

Magical Rank: Common Rank 1


Job(s): [None]


Stats:

Strength: 59
Agility: 31
Endurance: 82
Intelligence: 519



Jake then lost Consciousnesses
